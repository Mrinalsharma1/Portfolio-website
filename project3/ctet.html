CTET

Full form of CTET is ” Central Teacher Eligibility Test ” . CTET exam is Conducted by CBSE , basically the CTET Entrance Exam Organised on Every Year for Two Time February and September Month, and However the Number of eligible -candidate who -join Central Government School as KVS /NVS School as a Teacher Profile. A candidate has to score over 60 per cent to clear the eligibility test.Approximately 9 lac applicants appear for the examination in 2016.The examination is tough with qualification rates of 1% to 14% in the exams .What is CTET and Who is Eligible for CTET. TET was introduced by the Government of India in order to improve standards in teaching. It was held for the first time in 2011. For teachers already working, they are supposed to clear the exam in two years time . I wish now you know What is CTET and Who is Eligible for CTET

Why CTET ?

The purpose of CTET is fulfil and achieve the goals of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act. As well best teacher provide to our education. The nation’s growth depend on that student education level and moral value . And the best teacher provide the greatest education of our student . In other words we say That CTET is the best way to test our teacher.

Who is Eligible for CTET ?

Anyone who is Indian and want to contribute in Education.The following qualification should also be accompanied.

Eligibility criteria for becoming a teacher for class 1st to 5th.

Candidate must be Passed Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) with not less than 50% marks and passed or pursuing in last year of two-year Diploma in Elementary Education. OR, Completed Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) with not less than 45% marks and passed or pursuing in last year of two-year Diploma in Elementary Education as per NCTE regulations 2002. OR, Passed Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) examination with a minimum of 50% marks and qualified or pursuing in last year of four-year Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.El.Ed). OR, Passed Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) examination with not less than 50% marks and completed or pursuing in final year of Diploma in Education (two years duration) OR, Completed Graduation and cleared or pursuing in the final year of Diploma in Elementary Education (two years).

Eligibility criteria for becoming a teacher for class 6th to 8th.

Completed Graduation in any stream and cleared or pursuing in the final year of Diploma in Elementary Education (2-year). OR Completed Graduation with not less than 50% marks and cleared or pursuing in Bachelor in Education (1-year duration). OR, Completed Graduation with minimum of 45% marks and cleared or pursuing in Bachelor in Education (1-year duration) as per the NCTE rules and Regulations released from time to time in this regard. OR, Completed Senior Secondary or its equivalent examination with not less than 50% marks and cleared or pursuing in last year of Bachelor in Elementary Education (4- year duration). OR, Completed Senior Secondary or its equivalent examination with not less than 50% marks and cleared or pursuing in the final year of B.Sc.Ed/ B.A or B.Sc.Ed/ B.A.Ed (4- year duration). OR, Completed Graduation with not less than 50% marks and cleared or pursuing in B.Ed. (1-year).
